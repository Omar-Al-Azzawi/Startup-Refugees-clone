Get Involved

Startup Refugees in Finland consists of more than 1500 members, including companies, government officials, NGOs, universities, congregations, research institutes, communities and individuals who support newcomers with starting businesses and entering the Finnish labour market. Everyone is welcome to join the network, as long as they offer something concrete to support our common goal.
